About Us


The name St Croix Servant was derived by combining the geographical area we serve and and a subtle statement of our faith. Christ being the perfect Servant.

St Croix Servant was created to accomplish two things, First I wanted to provide for my family while serving the local community. Secondly it is a mechanism for me to teach my adopted children a trade and for them to be productive members of society.

My wife and I provide foster care for the Twin Cities metro area, we decided that we would adopt any child needing a family. We have since adopted five children and counting.

I have over two decades of experience in the Landscaping/Lawncare industry. We use the very best equipment and provide excellent service with the customer's needs always taking priority.
